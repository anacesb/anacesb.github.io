I am a PhD candidate in [Biomedical Engineering](https://bme.umich.edu/) and [Scientific Computing](https://micde.umich.edu/academic-programs/phd-in-scientific-computing/) at the [University of Michigan - Ann Arbor](https://umich.edu), working under the guidance of [Dr. Nicole Seiberlich](https://medschool.umich.edu/profile/5834/nicole-seiberlich) at the [Michigan Institute for Imaging Technology and Translation (MIITT)](https://miitt.med.umich.edu/home). My research focuses on cardiac MRI, including pulse sequence development, non-Cartesian image reconstruction, quantitative imaging, and machine learning for medical image analysis.


Short biography
---------------

I received my Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering and Master’s degree in Digital Signal and Image Processing from [Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú](https://www.pucp.edu.pe/). My broader research interests include signal and image processing, medical imaging systems, image acquisition and reconstruction, quantitative tissue characterization, and artificial intelligence for healthcare.
